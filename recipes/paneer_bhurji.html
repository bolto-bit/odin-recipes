Paneer Bhurji

The one vegetable dish every kid loved, no matter where.

Ingridients you need

  • Paneer
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic Paste
  • Garam Masala, Red Chilli Powder
  • Green Capsicum(optional)

Recipe

  1. Add oil and cumin seeds on a pan.
  2. Transfer onion once and fry once they start chaning color.
  3. Add garlic paste and fry well.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, salt, and turmeric(for color).
  5. Add garam masala, red chilli powder.
  6. Mix well and crumble the paneer before adding it to the pan.
