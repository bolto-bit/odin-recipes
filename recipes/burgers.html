Burgers

Burgers, an all time classic.

Burgers are yum.

Ingridients

  • Ground Chicken
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Onions, garlic
  • Egg
  • Seasonings
  • Oil
  • Lettuce, Tomatoes and others are optional

Steps to follow

  1. Mix ground chicken, bread crumbs, onions, garlic and season with salt and pepper
  2. Shape mixture in oval shaped patties
  3. Spread remaining crumbs in shallow dish. Dip patties in crumbs to coat
  4. Heat olive oil over medium-heat
  5. Cook patties in oil until well browned bottoms, around 5-6 minutes
  6. Do this with both sides of patties and serve
