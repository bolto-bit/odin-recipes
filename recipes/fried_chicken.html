Fried Chicken

The most delicious form of chicken

Ingridients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • Buttermilk
  • All-purpose flour
  • Paprika
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps to follow

  1. Season flour with salt pepper and paprika and seal in large plastic bag.
  2. Dip chicken in buttermilk and trasnfer to plastic bag and shake to coat well.
  3. Place chicken in tray and cover with waxed paper or towel.
  4. Let it sit until flour has paste like consistency.
  5. Fill large skillet with oil and let it heat until its very hot.
  6. Put as many chicken as fillet can hold.
  7. Brown from all sides in the hot oil.
  8. After browning, lower the heat and let it sit for 30 mins.
  9. Remove cover, heat again and let it crisp.
  10. Drain the fried chicken on paper towels and keep it in over while prepparing the rest.
